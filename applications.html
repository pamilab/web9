About the Research Team

A student-led initiative driven by accessibility, innovation, and applied radar research.

The Story Behind This Research

This FMCW-GPR prototype was developed by a group of Electronics Engineering students motivated by the limitations of commercially available subsurface radar systems. High costs and restricted access inspired the team to design a research-grade yet affordable solution that could support academic study, field experimentation, and future robotic integration. The project reflects a balance between theoretical signal processing principles and practical hardware implementation.
