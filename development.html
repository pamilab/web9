Building the Prototype

From concept and simulation to hardware integration and validation.

1. Concept and Planning

The project began with identifying system limitations in existing GPR designs and selecting FMCW radar as the optimal solution.

2. Design and Simulation

Radar equations, chirp characteristics, and depth-to-frequency relationships were analyzed to predict system performance.

3. Hardware Integration

RF modules, control units, and digitization hardware were assembled into a unified radar front-end.

4. Software Development

Signal acquisition and processing were implemented using GNU Radio and Python.
