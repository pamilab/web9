Measurement Consistency

Depth estimation was evaluated using 10 repeated trials per reference depth (n = 10). Results demonstrate stable FMCW chirp generation, consistent beat-frequency extraction, and reliable STFT-based signal processing under controlled laboratory conditions.

n = 10 trials @ 29 cm n = 10 trials @ 72 cm

Figure: Error per trial at a reference depth of 29 cm. Slight fluctuations are attributed to antenna coupling variations and surface reflections; however, all measurements remain within acceptable error bounds.

Overall Result: Experimental validation confirms that the 2.4 GHz ISM-band FMCW-GPR system achieves accurate, repeatable depth estimation suitable for controlled subsurface imaging and future UGV integration.
