Building the Prototype

From concept and simulation to hardware integration and validation

1. Concept and Planning

Defining system goals, constraints, and FMCW radar feasibility.

System limitations, frequency selection, regulatory compliance, and cost constraints were evaluated to guide architecture design.

2. Design and Simulation

Predicting performance using radar equations and models.

Simulations verified chirp bandwidth, beat frequency behavior, depth resolution, and expected signal response.

3. Hardware Integration

RF modules, SDR, antennas, and power systems were integrated into a stable mechanical assembly.

4. Software & STFT Processing

GNU Radio and Python enabled digitization, STFT processing, and radargram visualization.

5. Testing & Validation

Controlled experiments with metallic targets validated system depth accuracy against manual measurements.
