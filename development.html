FMCW Chirp Signal

Simulated frequency-swept waveform used to estimate target range.

Mathematical modeling and simulation of FMCW radar behavior.

Equation Expression Description
Chirp Signal s(t) = A cos(2π(f₀t + ½St²)) Time-domain expression of the transmitted FMCW chirp, where f₀ is the start frequency and S is the chirp slope.
Beat Frequency fb = S · τ Frequency difference between transmitted and received signals caused by propagation delay τ.
Propagation Delay τ = 2R / v Two-way travel time of the radar signal, where R is the target depth and v is wave velocity in the medium.
Depth Estimation R = (v · fb) / (2S) Relationship used to estimate subsurface target depth from the measured beat frequency.
Range Resolution ΔR = c / (2B) Depth resolution determined by the chirp bandwidth B.
