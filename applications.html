About the Research Team

The people, vision, and collaboration behind the FMCW-GPR prototype.

Our Story

This research began with the shared observation that most commercial ground-penetrating radar systems remain inaccessible due to high cost and operational complexity. As electronics engineering researchers, we aimed to bridge this gap by developing a compact and affordable FMCW-GPR prototype using commercially available components. The project was driven by the goal of combining solid theoretical design with practical system implementation, enabling reliable subsurface sensing for academic and field-based applications.
